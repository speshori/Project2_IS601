This is the story of how the internet has changed society and how a new breed of entrepreneurs are shaping our digital futures. As John Heilemann in the video says, “By now we all know the web has changed the world, but this is the story of how the world is changing the web.” It was termed as Web 2.0 time when the World Wide Web sites emphasized on user generated content and social networking. Towards the end of the episode, the Founder of Craigslist says: “The web is not just about making money, it’s about setting communication free.”
